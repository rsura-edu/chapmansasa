About Us

We are Chapman South Asian Student Association (SASA), a cultural club on campus dedicated to promoting South Asian heritage and fostering an inclusive environment for all. Our club goes beyond just celebrating South Asian culture; we strive to create a genuine, safe space for everyone interested in exploring the diverse cultures of South Asia. We're not exclusive to South Asians. Whether you have a direct connection to South Asia or simply have an interest in learning more about its various regions, traditions, and languages, you are more than welcome to be part of our community. Our events and activities encompass a range of cultural experiences, from traditional performances to thought-provoking discussions. Please explore our site and contact us if you would like to know more!
